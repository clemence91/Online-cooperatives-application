Vision and Mission

Rwanda Cooperative Agency (RCA) is a public institution which is in charge
of promotion, Registration and Regulation of Cooperatives in the Country

VISION :

To promote an automous and economically viable cooperative movement founded on the cooperative values and principles and is able to enhance social integration and uplifting the standard of living of its members.

MISSION :

To develop the cooperative sector such as it serves its members equitably,
efficiently and empowers them economically
